Our Website FAQ

Can I watch matches live?

Unfortunately we are unable to stream live video coverage of our teams matches. However, we do provide extended matchday highlights and full match summary stats of each game (uploaded as 2 separate halves).

How can I register my interest to join as a sponsor?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • Click the "Finance" tab.
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and you will see a description on sponsorship.
  • Click the "Click Here" button.
  • Try temporarily disabling any firewall software you have installed.
  • Try a different browser.
  • Clear the browser cache and reload the page.

You can additionally Click here to let us know of your interest.

How can I register my club's interest to join Ligi Open?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page on the footer.
  • Under "More" click "Register Club".
  • Fill in the club details and submit.

You can additionally Click here to register your club's interest.

Why can't I view any images?

Before contacting us, there are a few things you can try:

  • Refresh the website.
  • Try loading the page on a different network or check for internet connectivity issues.
  • If you have AdBlock, don't run it on our website.
  • Try an incognito / private window.
  • Try temporarily disabling any firewall software you have installed.
  • Try a different browser.
  • Clear the browser cache and reload the page.

If you still can't see images, let us know.

Why is the website loading slowly?

Before contacting us, there are a few things you can try:

  • Check your internet connection speed.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • Try accessing the website during off-peak hours.

If you still find the website loading slowly, let us know.
