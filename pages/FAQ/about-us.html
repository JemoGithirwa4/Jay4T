About Us FAQ

What is Ligi Open?

Ligi Open is a platform dedicated to providing funding and support to local grassroots football clubs in Kenya. Our aim is to develop and sustain football in Kenya through Open Financing which helps in ensuring financial support, transparency and community involvement.

How can I contact Ligi Open?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • Click the "Contact Us" tab.
  • Fill in the needed details with your message and click "Submit".
  • Wait for a reply, we will reach back to you.

Alternatively, you can email us at support@ligiopen.com.

Where is Ligi Open located?

Currently we do not have a physical address but we will notify you once we set up one.
