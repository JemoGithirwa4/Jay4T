Support & Troubleshooting FAQ

How do I report a bug on the website?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • Click the "Contact Us" tab.
  • Fill in the needed details, provide detailed information about the issue and click "Submit".
  • Wait for a reply, we will reach back to you.

Alternatively, you can email us at support@ligiopen.com.

Why am I not receiving email notifications?

Follow the following steps:

  • Check your spam or junk folder.
  • Ensure your email address is correctly entered when contacting us.
