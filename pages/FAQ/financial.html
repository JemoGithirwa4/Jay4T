Financial Transactions FAQ

How do I make a donation on the website?

Follow the following steps:

  • Head back to the home page by clicking on the logo at the top.
  • Click the "Finance" tab.
  • Click the "Contribute Now" button.
  • Select your payment method and proceed with the provided instructions.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information. Our website is SSL certified, ensuring all transactions are secure.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, credit/debit cards, checks, PayPal, Apple Pay, bank wire transfers, stock transfers, and private equity shares.

What are crowdfunding campaigns?

These are campaigns where fans can directly contribute financially to specific projects or initiatives within the league. The platform provides tools for transparent fundraising, allowing donors to track how their contributions are being used.
